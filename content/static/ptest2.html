Highlight of May: MAIFEST is coming

Do you know what is Maibock means? Maibock is the typical Bavarian spring beer with a full-body flavor and more. Mai s the German word for May. Bock means billy-goat and stands for the strength of the beer. Almost all labels carry a bock symbol.

Maibock is a dark top fermented beer like the English stout. Having been stronger brewed, it is better-tasting, intoxicating and keeps particularly well. This beer is produced with Munich malt, has a moderate amount of Hallertauer hops. In Bavaria, Maibock is available only in limited quantities, and are customarily served in the spring and are oftentimes interrelated with spring festivals and celebrations more often in the month of May.

Good news! You don't have to go to Germany to taste Maibock. Lets celebrate Maifest at the Paulaner with special beer freshly brewed by in house brewmaster called "Limited Maibock Billy Goat Beer". While you are enjoying the Maibock, tantalise your hunger with delectables and exquisite white asparagus menu prepared in 6 different ways. Lets get party started, folks!
